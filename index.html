SEPTEMBER 21, 2016-- We had planned our flight carefuly so that we would arrive in the Philippines before sunrise on September 23, our sister's birthday. Months before the trip, Mom told Ate that she would come home. Little did Ate know that her 3 siblings would be coming home too. It was a surprise that even we could barely handle and almost revealed multiple times. This would be the first time we'd be celebrating her birthday with her since 2009.
